Facts about the Red River rebellion:

  1. The Red River Rebellion lasted from 1869-1870

  2. It occurred in the Red River Colony(current day Winnipeg)

  3. The rebellion happened because the residents of the Red River Colony felt that the Canadian Government was threatening their culture and their land.

  4. The uprising led to the creation of the Province of Manitoba.

  5. After the Red River Rebellion, the Metis found themselves at a disadvantage and proceeded to move farther west and assemble for the northwest resistance.

Interview with Thomas Scott:

Thomas Scott is a resident of the Red River Settlement and a member of the Canadian party (a far-right party that is made up of mainly local ultra-Protestant agitators). We caught him outside the pub to ask him what his thoughts were on the situation surrounding the Metis.

What are your thoughts on the situation with the Metis provisional government?

“What have we come to when a bunch of half-breeds are telling us what to do I for one hate it.”

How do you think the Federal government should handle the Metis?

“We need to educate these Indian half-breeds so they stay inline”

What do you think the best course of action for the provisional government should be?

“They need to step down and give the government control to the Canadian party”

What are your thoughts on the Canadian party?

“I think they are a party that speaks for the pure white men trying to make a living on the Prarie. They are without a doubt better than those dirty Half breeds controlling us.”

Interview with Louis Riel:

We also interviewed Metis Provisional government leader Louis Riel the same questions here were his answers:

What are your thoughts on the situation with the Metis provisional government?

“I think it is great that finally a government is not made up of just rich white men"

How do you think the Federal government should handle the Metis?

“They should treat us like any other person we are equal to any white man”

What do you think the best course of action for the provisional government should be?

“We will continue to fight for the rights of all Metis people so that one day we could have the same rights as the white man”

What are your thoughts on the Canadian party?

“They are a bunch of Hayseed extremists who don’t deserve to live in the Red River Colony”
